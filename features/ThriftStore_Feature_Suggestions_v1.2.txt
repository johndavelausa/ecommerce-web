## ADMIN DASHBOARD — MISSING METRICS TO ADD

### 1. Total Sales
- Show total sales amount across all sellers platform-wide
- Display as a currency card (e.g. Total Sales: ₱24,500)
- Clicking it goes to the full sales breakdown in reports

### 2. Total Orders
- Show total number of orders placed across all sellers
- Display as a count card (e.g. Total Orders: 142)
- Clicking it goes to order list filtered to all statuses

### 3. Order Status Breakdown
- Show a mini breakdown of orders by status
- Processing: [count]
- Shipped: [count]
- Delivered: [count]
- Cancelled: [count]

### 4. Rejected Sellers
- Show count of sellers with status = rejected
- Display alongside Total Registered Sellers
- Example: Total Sellers: 30 | Rejected: 4

### 5. Bad Orders
- Definition: orders that were cancelled, returned, or failed to deliver
- Show count and percentage of total orders
- Example: Bad Orders: 8 (5.6% of total)

### 6. Churn Rate
- Definition: percentage of sellers who did not renew their subscription in the last month
- Formula: (sellers who lapsed this month / total active sellers last month) x 100
- Display as a percentage card
- Example: Seller Churn Rate: 12% this month

## SELLER DASHBOARD — MISSING METRICS TO ADD

### 1. Revenue
- Show total revenue earned from completed orders
- Display as a currency card separate from Total Sales
- Revenue = sum of (price_at_purchase x quantity) for all delivered orders

### 2. Net Profit
- Definition: Revenue minus any discounts or sale price differences
- Formula: sum of (sale_price x qty) for delivered orders — if no sale price, use regular price
- Display as a currency card
- Example: Net Profit: ₱8,200

### 3. Pending Orders
- Show count of orders with status = processing
- Display as a clickable card that links to order list filtered by processing

### 4. Orders to Ship
- Show count of orders with status = processing that are ready to be marked as shipped
- Same as Pending Orders count — just label it clearly as "Needs Shipping"

### 5. Completed Orders
- Show count of orders with status = delivered
- Display as a clickable card

### 6. Cancelled Orders
- Show count of orders with status = cancelled
- Display as a clickable card

### 7. Bad Orders
- Definition: cancelled + any orders reported as problematic by the customer
- Show count and percentage
- Example: Bad Orders: 3 (4.1% of your total orders)

### 8. Out of Stock Items
- Already tracks low stock (below 10)
- Add a separate count for products where stock = 0
- Display two separate cards:
  - Low Stock: [count] products (stock below 10)
  - Out of Stock: [count] products (stock = 0)

### 9. Customer Ratings & Reviews (on dashboard)
- Show average store rating across all products
- Example: Store Rating: 4.3 / 5 (from 28 reviews)
- Clicking it links to the full reviews page

## DATABASE NOTES

### Admin — new queries needed
- Total Sales: SELECT SUM(total_amount) FROM orders WHERE status = 'delivered'
- Total Orders: SELECT COUNT(*) FROM orders
- Order status counts: SELECT status, COUNT(*) FROM orders GROUP BY status
- Rejected sellers: SELECT COUNT(*) FROM sellers WHERE status = 'rejected'
- Bad orders: SELECT COUNT(*) FROM orders WHERE status = 'cancelled'
- Churn rate: compare sellers with lapsed subscription_status this month vs last month

### Seller — new queries needed
- Revenue: SELECT SUM(price_at_purchase * quantity) FROM order_items JOIN orders WHERE orders.seller_id = ? AND orders.status = 'delivered'
- Net Profit: same as revenue but using sale_price if available
- Pending orders: SELECT COUNT(*) FROM orders WHERE seller_id = ? AND status = 'processing'
- Completed orders: SELECT COUNT(*) FROM orders WHERE seller_id = ? AND status = 'delivered'
- Cancelled orders: SELECT COUNT(*) FROM orders WHERE seller_id = ? AND status = 'cancelled'
- Out of stock: SELECT COUNT(*) FROM products WHERE seller_id = ? AND stock = 0
- Average rating: SELECT AVG(rating) FROM reviews JOIN products WHERE products.seller_id = ?
